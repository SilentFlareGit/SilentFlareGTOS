Germany and EU Compliance

Because SilentFlare infrastructure may be hosted in Germany or the European Union, users must comply with German law, European Union law, sanctions rules, export-control rules, data-protection rules, and lawful abuse-prevention requirements.

User responsibility

  • Do not use the service to violate local, German, EU, or international law.
  • Do not attempt to bypass sanctions, export controls, court orders, or access restrictions.
  • Provide truthful account, billing, and verification information when required.

Provider rights

SilentFlare may restrict access, request verification, retain abuse evidence, respond to lawful requests, or terminate service when required to reduce legal or infrastructure risk.
