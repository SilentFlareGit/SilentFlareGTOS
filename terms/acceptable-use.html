Acceptable Use Policy

Users may only use SilentFlare in lawful, responsible, and non-abusive ways. The following activity is prohibited.

  • Spam, phishing, credential theft, impersonation, or social engineering.
  • Malware, botnets, exploit hosting, command-and-control, or unauthorized scanning.
  • Denial-of-service attacks, traffic amplification, brute force, or abuse of shared infrastructure.
  • Copyright infringement, illegal content, harassment, doxxing, or threats.
  • Attempts to evade sanctions, export controls, law-enforcement restrictions, or regional controls.
  • Activity that harms SilentFlare, networks, users, third parties, or public internet stability.

Abuse reports

SilentFlare may investigate abuse reports, preserve evidence, rate-limit traffic, disable resources, or require additional information from the user.
